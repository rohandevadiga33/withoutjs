Hands-on technical education, since 1985

Established in 1985, Dr. T.M.A. Pai Polytechnic Manipal has developed into one of the best polytechnic colleges in India. Part of the renowned academic township of Manipal, Dr. T.M.A. Pai polytechnic is closely associated with some of the most reputed colleges in the country.

We are approved and recognised by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi (AICTE) and affiliated to the Board of Technical Education, Bangalore. Situated on a large, independent campus, we have been catering to the local population of Udupi and Dakshina Kannada districts as well as students from other parts of the state and country.
